Our Vision

In the last few decades, scientists and engineers have made incredible technological advances such as online payment systems, centralized work communications, virtual homes and much more. However, the internet is not always safe, and cyberattacks are on the rise. We want to foster a community that helps students explore their interests in cybersecurity through activities, networking, and collaboration.
